Add Meetings from Slush Matchmaking to Your Calendar

Here's an easy way to get all the meetings from Slush Matchmaking to your own calendar. This bookmarklet will create an export-file from Slush Matchmaking, which you can then import to your favorite calendar tool.

Slush Matchmaking exportStart by dragging me to your bookmarks

1. Install the bookmarklet

You can just drag & drop the button to your bookmarks bar in the browser. It's that simple!

2. Go to Slush Matchmaking

Navigate to the list of all your meetings.

3. Export all your meetings

Click the bookmarklet to download an export of all your meetings

4. Add meetings to calendar

You can easily import the file to your calendar
